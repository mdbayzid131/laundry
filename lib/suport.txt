Socket implementation Plan: Support tickets & Notifications
This guide focuses on the technical integration of real-time Support Tickets and personalized Notifications.

1. Prerequisites
Library: socket.io-client
Authentication: Token must be passed in the auth object during connection.
Registration: You must emit register with the userId immediately after connecting to receive notifications.

B. Usage Scenarios
Badge Updates: Update the notification bell count in the header.
Push Alerts: Show an in-app toast or snackbar when a notification arrives.
Navigation: If the notification has a specific type (e.g., ORDER_UPDATE), you can deep-link the user to the relevant screen.
4. Troubleshooting Checklist
Not receiving notifications? Ensure you emitted socket.emit("register", userId) after connection.
Messages not appearing? Ensure you emitted socket.emit("join-room", roomId) when opening the ticket.
Double messages? Ensure you remove the listener or avoid re-joining the room multiple times on component re-renders.
